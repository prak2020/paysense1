About Flywire — The University Standard
Accepted by 3,000+ universities worldwide · Built specifically for international education payments
Flywire is the most widely accepted student payment platform globally, trusted by thousands of Indian students every year. Most universities have Flywire payment links built directly into their fee portals, making it the default and most frictionless path for tuition payments. Flywire offers end-to-end payment tracking, instant receipt confirmations, FX risk protection, and multi-currency support — purpose-built for the complexity of international education payments.

Beyond the headline exchange rate spread, sending money through Indian banks and ADIIs typically involves a stack of additional charges that quietly inflate your total cost: SWIFT/wire transfer fees (₹500–₹1,500 per transaction), correspondent bank charges (USD 15–35 deducted at the destination before the university receives funds), and documentary handling or service fees levied by the remitting branch. These charges are often disclosed only in the fine print — or discovered after the money has moved. Flywire's all-inclusive, transparent pricing eliminates all of these surprises: no SWIFT deductions at the university end, no correspondent bank skimming, no hidden service charges — the amount you initiate is the amount the university credits, making it the most reliable and student-friendly option for tuition payments globally.

💡 Smart Tips for Indian Students
🎓
Flywire pays your institution directly
Flywire routes funds directly to your university's account — reducing the risk of delays, rejections, or misapplied payments.
💸
The spread gap narrows at higher amounts
Flywire's markup compresses from ~2.4% at ₹5,000 to under 1.5% at ₹25,000 for some currencies. At larger tuition amounts, the cost difference vs. banks shrinks considerably — always check the right band.
🏦
Bank TT rates carry hidden variables
Bank TT Sell rates are set once a day by 9:30 AM IST. They exclude wire transfer fees (₹500–₹2,000 per transaction) and correspondent bank charges (USD 15–35) deducted before funds reach your university — costs that don't appear in the rate comparison above.
📅
LRS limit: $250,000 per year per person
RBI's Liberalised Remittance Scheme allows up to USD 2,50,000 per financial year. From April 1, 2026, education remittances above ₹10L attract 2% TCS. Loan-funded remittances under Section 80E attract 0% TCS — all TCS is refundable at ITR filing. Flywire has built a proprietary TCS calculation algorithm that automatically computes your exact TCS liability at checkout,
🔒
Flywire locks your rate at payment
When you initiate a Flywire payment, the INR amount is locked in real time — eliminating the risk of the rate moving between your bank visit and the actual wire execution, which can take 1–2 business days.
📋
Compare total cost, not just the rate
A 1% spread difference on a ₹10L tuition transfer is ₹10,000. Factor in your bank's wire fee, correspondent deductions, and the risk of a payment rejection before deciding which channel gives you the best net cost.
